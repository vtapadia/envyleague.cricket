General Rules
  • This is a site purely for learning purpose and the owner has no commercial interest.
  • This is intended only for friends and family. So if you don't know the owner, this is not the place.
  • This site is running on a shared server, with some downtime. So please place your predictions well in time.
  • No backend updates will be entertained.
Fantasy Leagues
  • As a user, you have to play in a League.
  • You can go to the All League section and register in a League where you know the League owner.
  • You can request for a New League. However, this is subject to approval from Admin.
  • The fee for a league is just to give a fun element and is purely up to the league owner. Owner can choose this value to be 0.
  • Once a League is approved, your other friends can join your league and as a League owner, you can approve their request in Manage League section.
Fantasy Prediction
  • The predictions are on the total match.
  • The final values will only consider the 50 overs (and not any super over).
  • The match winner is decided based on 50 overs and not on any toss or super over.
  • The final decision rest with the Admin.
Fantasy Scoring
  • The correct final result of the match will give you 10 points.
  • For the Total(Runs/Sixes/Fours/Wickets), if final score falls in your predictions range, 5 points will be awarded.
  • These points will be multiplied by 2 for Quarter Finals, 3 for Semi Finals and 4 for Finals
