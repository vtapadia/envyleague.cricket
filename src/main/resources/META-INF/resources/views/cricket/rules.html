General Rules
  • This is a site purely for learning purpose and the owner has no commercial interest.
  • This is purely for friends and family. So if you don't know the owner, don't register.
Fantasy Leagues
  • As a user, you play in a League.
  • You can request for a new League. However, this is subject to approval from Admin/Owner.
  • The fee for a league is just to give a fun element and is purely up to the league owner. Owner can choose this value to be 0.
  • Once a League is approved, your other friends can join your league and you can approve the request of them.
Fantasy Prediction
  • The predictions are on the total match.
  • The final values will only consider the 50 overs (and not any super over).
  • The match winner is decided based on 50 overs and not on any toss or super over.
  • The final decision rest with the Admin.
Fantasy Scoring
  • The correct final result of the match will give you 10 points.
  • For the Total(Runs/Wickets/Fours/Sixes), if any one in the league gets an exact match, 10 points will be given
  • if no one in the league, gets an exact match for Total(Runs/Wickets/Fours/Sixes), the nearest one will get 5 bonus points.
  • These points will be multiplied by 2 for Quarter Finals, 3 for Semi Finals and 4 for Finals
